Steps:

  1. Understand the Rules:
    • Rock crushes Scissors
    • Scissors cut Paper
    • Paper covers Rock
  2. Setup: Make fists and count to three together.
  3. Throw Signs: On three, show Rock (fist), Paper (open hand), or Scissors (V).
  4. Determine Winner: Compare signs. Same sign means a tie.
  5. Winning: Decide on best of three, five, or first to a set wins.
